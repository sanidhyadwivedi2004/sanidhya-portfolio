The Best Movie According To Sanidhya

My Top 3 Movies of all time

Fighter

A reckless yet brilliant squadron leader and his team of elite fighter pilots face mortal dangers and inner demons as they unite for a deadly mission.

Chamkila

Amar Singh Chamkila was an Indian singer and musician of Punjabi music. Chamkila's vivid language, high-pitched vocals, and novel compositions accompanied by tumbi made him popular. His music was influenced by the Punjabi village life in which he grew up.

Pippa

Balram Singh Mehta steps up to prove his worth by fighting on the eastern front during the Indo-Pakistani war of 1971.
